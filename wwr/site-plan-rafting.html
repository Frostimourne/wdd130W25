Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this site is to champion the voice of Rustic Whitewater Incorporated. RWI is committed to bringing our passion for bringing the sport of whitewater rafting to everyone, from the seasoned pros to the novice buying their first kayak. Through robust resources focused on educating our customers about both thrills and safety, showing them the best whitewater rafting locations near them, and providing the best catalogue of equipment for their next adventure, Rustic Whitewater Incorporated will be the premier whitewater rafting site and company in the United States!

Audience

The intended audience of RWI is any and all who love the outdoors -- whether they have the time to get out for a week or just a weekend. Adventurers are a hungry and thrill-seeking people. We know it can be difficult to find new spots to go to to justify the cost of equipment. This is where RWI will provide the valuable information and access to increase investment in the hobby. With a robust site for desktop viewing and planning at home matched with an unparalleled mobile experience for those already up the canyon, RWI will be the one-stop shop for all outdoor adventurers
